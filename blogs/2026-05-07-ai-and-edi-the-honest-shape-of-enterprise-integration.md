I have been pulling the Naftiko Signals data this week looking at what enterprises are actually investing in right now — what shows up in their job postings, their press releases, the technology footprint we score across their public surfaces. The picture that came back from the dataset is honest in a way that most analyst slides are not. The top of the investment chart, sorted by company adoption across the 253 enterprises in the Signals dataset, is not “AI plus a bunch of modern things.” It is AI and EDI, side by side, at the very top.

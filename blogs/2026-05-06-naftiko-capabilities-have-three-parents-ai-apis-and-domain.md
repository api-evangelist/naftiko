I keep getting asked where Naftiko Capabilities came from. The honest answer is that they came from three places at once, and most of the conversation about agents in 2026 is only willing to talk about two of them. The two everyone is comfortable with are AI and APIs. AI gave us the runtime — the model, the tool-calling loop, MCP, the agent skill manifest, the whole shape of “the model can call something to get work done.” APIs gave us the wire format — REST, GraphQL, gRPC, JSON over HTTP, the pile of legacy SOAP and CSV that nobody is rewriting. Both of those are well-rehearsed parents.

Roadmaps are buyer documents. The platform team reads them to know whether to pilot now or wait. The security team reads them to know whether the right governance hooks are coming. The CFO reads them to know whether the cost picture stabilizes before the next budget cycle. The Head of AI reads them to know whether the AI integration story holds together at production scale. The Naftiko roadmap covers four products — Naftiko Framework, Naftiko Framework Wiki, Naftiko Fleet, Naftiko Fleet Wiki — across five milestones from First Alpha to General Availability.

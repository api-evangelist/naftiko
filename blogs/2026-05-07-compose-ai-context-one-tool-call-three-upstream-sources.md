I am still watching the same pattern play out every time a team tries to get an agent to answer a real business question. The agent calls one tool, then another, then a third, then tries to stitch the three responses together in a prompt. Sometimes it works. Often it does not. Always it is expensive. This is where the fifth Naftiko Framework use case earns its keep. Compose AI context. Combine data from multiple APIs into one capability to deliver richer, task-ready context to AI clients. Fifth post of nine. Same three-dimensional lens I have been using — technology, business, politics.

A pattern in enterprise IT keeps repeating, and we are watching it repeat right now in real time. A category opens up. A startup makes the entry point easy and free, gets people hooked at the team and individual level, and then funnels everyone toward the SaaS-cloud version where the actual revenue lives. The cloud version is the lock-in. By the time the enterprise notices, half of their teams are using it, and now the migration cost of leaving is bigger than the renewal price. I have watched that play happen for two decades. I have been on the side that benefited from it.
